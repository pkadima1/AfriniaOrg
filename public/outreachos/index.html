Six things OutreachOS does every day.

All inside your Google account. Nothing leaves your Drive.

1

Finds Prospects

Runs targeted searches by job title, industry, and location — automatically cycling through your ideal customer profile combinations.

2

Enriches Each Lead

Captures name, company, role, and LinkedIn URL for every prospect found — no manual research required.

3

Writes Personalised Emails

Uses AI to write a unique email for each prospect. Not a template with a name swapped in — a genuinely tailored message based on their role and company.

4

Sends via Gmail or Zoho Mail

Emails go out from your own dedicated business address — via Gmail or Zoho Mail. Your domain, your reputation, fully under your control.

Drafts LinkedIn DMs

Generates a matching LinkedIn message for each prospect — ready for you to review and send in a 10-minute daily queue.

Logs Everything

Every prospect, email, status, and reply date tracked automatically in your Google Sheet control room.
