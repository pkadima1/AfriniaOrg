Most Outreach Fails Before It Starts
You already know personalised outreach works. You've read the stats. You've seen the reply rates. The
problem isn't knowing — it's execution. Doing it manually, at any meaningful scale, is simply impossible
without sacrificing the quality that makes it work in the first place.

Recipients can spot a mail merge from the subject line. If your opening line could apply to
anyone, it will convert like it applies to no one.

message. Yet follow-ups are the first thing that slips in a busy week.

T H E S O L U T I O N
What OutreachOS Does
OutreachOS is a private automation tool that runs entirely inside your own Google account. There is no
external platform, no monthly subscription, no third-party holding your data. It sits quietly in the
background, sourcing leads, researching prospects, writing personalised messages, and sending them
on schedule — every single day, without you needing to think about it.
C O R E C A P A B I L I T I E S
Intelligent Lead Sourcing
Automatically finds and adds new prospects every day based on your exact target
audience — by role, industry, and location. No manual searching required.
Deep Prospect Research
For each prospect, the system visits their company website and extracts real signals —
growth announcements, product launches, hiring patterns — to create genuine
personalisation hooks.
AI-Written Personalised Messages
Every email is uniquely written by AI using the enrichment data. Not a template with a
name swapped in. An actual message that references something specific and real about
each prospect.
Automated Follow-Up Sequences
A structured 3-message cadence fires automatically: initial outreach, follow-up on day 3,
final message on day 8. Sequences stop the moment a reply is detected.
LinkedIn DM Drafting Queue
Personalised LinkedIn messages are drafted and queued for your review each morning.
You copy, paste, and send manually — keeping your account completely safe from
automation risks.
Full Campaign Tracking
Every action is logged. Every reply detected. Every status updated in real time. Your
Google Sheet is your live campaign dashboard — no separate CRM needed.

G E T T I N G S T A R T E D
Setup Guide — Step by Step
OutreachOS is set up once and then runs on its own. The process takes approximately 2–3 hours if
you're doing it yourself for the first time. Below is exactly what's involved, without the technical jargon.
1
Create your Google Sheet database
A template Sheet is provided with all the right tabs and column headers already in
place. You duplicate it into your own Google Drive. This Sheet becomes your
campaign's source of truth — prospects, statuses, settings, and activity logs all live
here.
2
Configure your Campaign Settings
Inside the Sheet, you fill in a simple form: who you're targeting (job titles, industries,
location), a description of your offer, your daily sending limits, and your email details. No
code. Just text fields.
3
Connect your API services
OutreachOS uses three external services to do its work. Each requires a free or lowcost account and an API key that you paste into a secure settings area. Step-by-step
instructions are provided for each one. These services are: an AI provider for message
writing, a search API for finding prospects, and an email service for sending.
4
Paste the code into Google Apps Script
Google Apps Script is Google's built-in automation tool — no installation required, it's
already inside your Google account. You create a new project, paste in the provided
code files (one per file, clearly labelled), and save.
5
Deploy as a Web App
With two clicks inside Apps Script, you convert the project into a private web application.
This gives you a URL you can open in any browser to access your OutreachOS
dashboard — showing today's queue, LinkedIn drafts, campaign stats, and recent
activity.
6
Install the automation schedule
You run one function called installTriggers(). This sets up three automated schedules: a
daily send batch at 9 AM, a reply check every 2 hours, and a follow-up processor at 10
AM. After this step, everything runs automatically.
7
Add test prospects and do a dry run
Before going live, you add 2–3 test rows to your Sheet and run the system manually to
verify everything is connecting correctly — enrichment runs, messages generate, emails
send. When it works cleanly, your daily automation is live.OutreachOS · NodeMatics.com Private Client Document — Not For Redistribution

C O M P L I A N C E & S A F E T Y
How It Stays Compliant
OutreachOS is built around two non-negotiable principles: email compliance and LinkedIn safety. Both
are baked into the architecture — not added as an afterthought.
Your data never leaves your Google account
All prospect data, campaign settings, and activity logs live in your personal Google Sheet.
OutreachOS doesn't have a central server. There is no third-party platform holding your
contacts or your IP.
Email sending via a dedicated business address
Outreach emails are sent through a separate business email account — not your
personal Gmail. This protects your main inbox reputation and keeps cold outreach
completely separate from your day-to-day communications.
LinkedIn is always human-approved
OutreachOS never automates LinkedIn sending. The system drafts the messages and
queues them — you send each one manually. This is the only approach that keeps your
LinkedIn account safe and within platform policy.
Automatic sequence stops on reply
The moment a prospect replies, their follow-up sequence is cancelled. No one receives a
follow-up after already responding. This is enforced automatically with no manual
intervention required.
Daily sending limits you control
You set your own daily volume cap. The system respects it strictly. This keeps sending
patterns natural and protects your sender reputation over time.OutreachOS · NodeMatics.com Private Client Document — Not For Redistribution
