## WHAT IS OUTREACHOS

OutreachOS is a done-for-you B2B outreach automation system that runs entirely inside Google Workspace (Google Sheets + Gmail + Google Apps Script).

It is NOT a SaaS subscription. The buyer pays ONCE and owns the system in their own Google Drive.

What it does (these are the 6 core capabilities — use them verbatim):
1. Finds qualified B2B prospects automatically using targeted search queries based on job title, industry, and location
2. Enriches each prospect with name, company, role, and LinkedIn profile URL
3. Generates a personalised outreach email for each prospect using AI — not templates
4. Sends the email from the buyer's own Gmail account
5. Drafts a matching LinkedIn DM for manual sending (respecting LinkedIn policies)
6. Logs everything in a Google Sheet — sent, replied, status, date

Key metrics to display prominently:
- 450+ email touches per month
- 240+ LinkedIn DM drafts per month
- ~10 minutes of daily effort
- Runs in YOUR Google account — no third-party data storage
- One-time payment — no monthly fees
